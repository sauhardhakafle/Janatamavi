Who are we?

We are Alumni of janata Ma.Vi. Danda. Janata Ma.Vi. Danda Alumni Association is an Non-profit,Non-governmental,Non-political organization established on 2078 with the purpose of helping in the field of education, health and community.
We are the help for those who needs.

Our purpose

This organization will carry out the role of helper as a volunteer in economic and charity programs in education, health and social sectors based on the capacity, needs, and priorities within its work area.
